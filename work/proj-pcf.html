48-Hour Project: Portland Casino Fridays

Problem

A local non-profit needed a website. A community of volunteer dance teachers wanted to advertise their services, but had little money and even less idea how to make a website.

Solution

Me, I guess. I know these teachers and I love making websites. I offered my services for a single weekend. In 48 hours I gathered requirements, did research, designed, and fully built Portland Casino Fridays' new, responsive website.

If you want to see the site for yourself you can visit Portland Casino Fridays as it's currently hosted on my server.
