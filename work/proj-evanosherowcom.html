EvanOsherow.com

Summary

I like clocks and watches. I also like creating pretty web things. So I decided to turn EvanOsherow.com into a "perpetutal calendar". A perpetual calendar is a special type of clock that not only tracks hours, minutes, and seconds, but the day, date, and current moon phase.

I made everything from the grapics to the HTML, CSS, and Javascript. It was a fun project because it was a challenge. It forced me to learn new skills and that was the most fulfilling part of all.
