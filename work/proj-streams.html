Webtrends Streams

Problem

Customers wanted live data. Historical analytics is useful, but live data is needed with the launch of a new campaign or even to assess problems. Webtrends customers wanted live customer data on both aggregate and individual levels.

Solution

  1. Product managers provided the User Experience team with a list of feature requirements including: live data viz dashboards, live customer flows, and campaign monitoring.
  2. We began on paper, sketching each feature. Sketches were viewed with the product development team and select customers.
  3. Full, pixel-level designs were created for each section and feature. The designs were then elaborated further outside of the User Experience team, especially with the development team.
  4. The product was built and launched as a beta.
  5. We received feedback from the beta customers and continued this cycle.

My Role

I was the primary designer on Webtrends Streams. From paper to Photoshop to CSS, I was responsible for visual design and interactions.
