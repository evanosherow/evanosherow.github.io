Astrograph Time Passages

Summary

In the nascent days of iOS app development I was hired by Astrograph Software to design their first iOS app. Taking several features from their desktop app I began with paper sketches moving through pixel-level designs. I worked with the iOS developer until the software's first release in late 2009.

Please remember, this was long ago. I show the app it here because I had so much fun making it.
