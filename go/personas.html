Webtrends User Personas

Problem

In order to design the best experience possible we needed to always have our users in mind. However, thinking of real and specific users allowed us to address only those users' needs.

Solution

  1. The User Experience team, with help from support and sales conducted extensive research into our users and who we want as users. We contacted dozens of customers and even some potential customers of all Webtrends products.
  2. Our research was compiled and analyzed for similarities and differences. We looked for recurring themes in users' responses.
  3. The research resulted in four distinct user personas and their respective stories. We elaborated and filled out each persona to make them more real and relatable.
  4. We designed user persona posters and placed them around the Webtrends office to help everyone keep in mind who they were building for.

My Role

I was heavily involved in user research: contacting customers, talking to our sales team, and collecting data from the support team. I helped analyze the user information and helped form the four personas. I was the Lead Designer on the persona posters featured here.
